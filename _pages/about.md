## About Me

I am a space scientist with research interests focused on planetary ionospheres, plasma and magnetic environments of Earth and planets, and space weather processes. My work aims to understand the electrodynamic coupling between the lower atmosphere, ionosphere, and magnetosphere across different spatial and temporal scales.

My research integrates ground-based observations, satellite measurements, and data-driven analysis techniques to investigate ionospheric variability, equatorial electrodynamics, and space weather impacts.

---

## Research Interests

- Planetary ionospheres (Earth & Mars)
- Atmosphere–ionosphere–magnetosphere coupling
- Equatorial and low-latitude ionospheric electrodynamics
- Plasma processes and space weather impacts
- Ground-based and satellite observations

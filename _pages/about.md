## About Me

I am a space scientist working on **planetary ionospheres**, **plasma and magnetic environments of Earth and planets**, and **space weather processes**.  
My research integrates **ground-based experiments**, **satellite observations**, and **data-driven techniques** to understand electrodynamic coupling across atmospheric and near-space regions.

---

## Research Interests

- Planetary ionospheres (Earth & Mars)  
- Atmosphere–ionosphere–magnetosphere coupling  
- Equatorial and low-latitude ionospheric electrodynamics  
- Plasma processes and space weather impacts  
- Ground-based (ionosonde, magnetometer, GPS-TEC) and satellite observations  

---
